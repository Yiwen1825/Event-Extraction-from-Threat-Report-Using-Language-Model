Liberty customers compromised in ransomware attack
Liberty Life has fallen victim to a ransomware attack, with the personal data of millions of the insurance company’s customers potentially at stake. | AttackRansom
Last night (16 June), the company informed customers that hackers have gained unauthorised access to its IT infrastructure. | O
“An external party claims to have seized data from us, has alerted us to potential vulnerabilities in our systems and has requested compensation for this,” a statement to customers reads. | AttackDatabreach
“Since becoming aware, we have taken immediate steps to secure our computer systems.” | O
It is believed that the hackers have demanded millions to prevent them from releasing sensitive data about clients. | AttackRansom
“Liberty is investigating the breach and we will endeavour to keep all stakeholders fully informed as appropriate,” the statement adds.  | O
“We are working hard to rectify the situation.”   | O
