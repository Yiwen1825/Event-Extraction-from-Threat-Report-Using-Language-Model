Adobe January Patch Tuesday Updates Released – No Security Updates For Flash!
This week, Adobe has  released its very first Patch Tuesday update bundle for the year 2019.  | PatchVulnerability
The Adobe January Patch Tuesday updates brought fixes for security vulnerabilities in Adobe Digital Editions and Adobe Connect. | PatchVulnerability
It has also released patches for Flash Player, but they are not security fixes. | PatchVulnerability
This Tuesday, Adobe has rolled-out scheduled monthly updates for its products. | O
However, this time, it has particularly focused on Adobe Digital Editions and Adobe Connect for security fixes. | PatchVulnerability
Besides, the update bundle is relatively smaller, unlike the previous updates that addressed tens of vulnerabilities. | PatchVulnerability
According to the security advisory, Adobe has fixed an important security vulnerability in Adobe Digital Editions. | PatchVulnerability
Describing the problem, they stated, | O
    “Successful exploitation could lead to information disclosure in the context of the current user.” | O
Reportedly, it’s an out of bounds read flaw (CVE-2018-12817) that affected the software version 4.5.9 and earlier for all platforms, i.e., Windows, MacOS, Android and iOS. | DiscoverVulnerability
Users should ensure updating their devices with the patched Adobe Digital Editions version 4.5.10. | PatchVulnerability
In addition to the above, another important vulnerability existed in Adobe Connect that could result in session token exposure. | DiscoverVulnerability
As stated in the advisory, the vulnerability (CVE-2018-19718) could “lead to exposure of privileges granted to a session.” | DiscoverVulnerability
The vulnerability affected the Adobe Connect versions 9.8.1 and earlier for all platforms. | O 
Users should, hence, ensure updating their systems with the patched version 10.1. |PatchVulnerability
Besides the two security fixes, Adobe have released patches for Flash Player as well addressing performance issues. | PatchVulnerability, DiscoverVulnerability
As described in the Adobe advisory, | O
    “Adobe has released updates for Adobe Flash Player for Windows, macOS, Linux and Chrome OS.  | PatchVulnerability
These updates address feature and performance bugs, and do not include security fixes.” | PatchVulnerability
The patched Flash Player version 32.0.0.114 has been rolled-out to be downloaded across all platforms. | PatchVulnerability
This time, the update bundle did not address security problems in Adobe Reader or Acrobat. | PatchVulnerability
However, the vendors already released security fixes for them in the previous week. | PatchVulnerability
The patch addressed two critical vulnerabilities (CVE-2018-16011 and CVE-2018-16018) that could result in arbitrary code execution and privilege escalation respectively. | PatchVulnerability
