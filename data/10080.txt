 UK arrests 2 suspected in DC police camera hacking  
United Kingdom authorities arrested two suspects in relation to January's hacking of Washington police surveillance cameras, International Business Times reported Friday. | O
The cyberattack affected 123 of the city's 187 network video recorders, city officials said, leaving the infected cameras unable to record between Jan 12 and Jan 15. | O
The National Crime Agency arrested a British man and Swedish woman, both 50, in a South London neighborhood, according to the report. | O 
The incident attracted particular attention because it came just days before President Trump's inauguration. | O 
The hack involved ransomware, a type of software that disables systems until demands are met. | AttackRansom 
Washington's chief technology officer said that no ransom was paid in this instance. | AttackRansom 
Instead, the city repaired damage from the attack on its own. | O 
City officials say the attack posed no risk to the public or the security of the inauguration. | O
